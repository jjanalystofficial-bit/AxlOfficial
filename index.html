About Us

Axl Garnish Fruits & Herbs is a newly established online company founded on April 21, 2023. On this day, we sold our first bag of dehydrated fruit.

We recognized a growing demand to help hospitality professionals reduce labor workload by providing ready-to-use garnishes with near-zero lead times.

Our business is committed to serving and producing high-quality garnishes and herbs at fair prices.
